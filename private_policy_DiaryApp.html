Privacy Policy

App: Diary Calendar - Daily Journal
Last updated: 11 January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Diary Calendar - Daily Journal ("the App") collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. Information We Collect

The App is designed to function primarily offline and does not require user accounts.

The App may store the following information locally on your device only:

  • Text diary entries created by the user
  • Photos manually selected by the user (optional feature)
  • App preferences (such as calendar view settings)

Important: This information is not transmitted to our servers and is not shared with the developer.

2. Advertising

The App displays advertisements using third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob).

Advertising partners may automatically collect limited information necessary to provide and measure ads, including:

  • Device information (such as advertising ID)
  • Approximate location (non-precise, country or region level)
  • Ad interaction data

This data is collected directly by the advertising provider, not by the App or the developer.

For more information, please refer to Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

The developer does not collect or store:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts, messages, or call logs
  • Biometric data

4. Photos and Media

Photos added to diary entries are:

  • Optional
  • Stored locally on the user's device
  • Never uploaded, shared, or backed up to any external server by the App

The App only accesses photos that the user explicitly selects.

5. Data Storage and Security

All diary content is stored locally on the user's device using the device's internal storage. The developer does not operate backend servers or cloud databases for user content. Users are responsible for device-level backups if desired.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. Advertisements shown in the App comply with Google Play's families and child safety policies where applicable.

7. Your Control Over Data

You may delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting diary entries within the App
  • Uninstalling the App from your device

Uninstalling the App permanently removes all locally stored data.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
